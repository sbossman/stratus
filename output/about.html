About Me

Hello! My name is Summer Bossman, and I am an undergraduate student in the University of Florida Honors program. I'm majoring in Computer Science, with a minor in English. I'll be graduating in 2026.

I am currently an Undergraduate Peer Mentor for Programming Fundamentals II (COP3503C). There, I teach students how to program in C++. I hold discussion section, where we review course content. I also host officer hours, where I debug student code.

In my free time, I enjoy programming, reading, cross stitch, writing, baking, and watching television. I've been keeping a daily journal since I was in 8th grade (so for over six years). I also currently have a sourdough starter named Harold, although I haven't had time to frequently bake with him. I've also folded over 5,000 paper cranes!
